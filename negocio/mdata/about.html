Mission Statement

Metadata is the biggest little problem plaguing the music industry. With our website, we make this process easy to write the underlying information tied to a released song or album, including titles, songwriter and producer names, the publisher(s), the record label, and more..

Our Story

MDTA started with producer Josh looking to improve the metadata process for the music industry. As a producer, he knew the importance of a good records so everybody in the record label can get paid of what they work for.
